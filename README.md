# Amazon-sales

# 🛒 Amazon India Sales Analysis

## Business Problem
Amazon India wants to understand product performance 
across categories to optimize pricing strategy, 
discount offerings, and product recommendations 
for maximum revenue and customer satisfaction.

## Dataset
- **Source:** Amazon India Product Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
- **Size:** 1,465 products
- **Categories Analyzed:** Electronics, 
  Computers&Accessories, Home&Kitchen, OfficeProducts

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Key Business Questions Answered
1. Which categories generate highest revenue potential?
2. Does discount % affect customer ratings?
3. Which categories have best rated products?
4. What discount range leads to highest ratings?
5. Which products offer best value to customers?

## Key Findings
- **Electronics** drives the highest revenue potential
- **Discount % has weak negative correlation (-0.16)** 
  with ratings — heavy discounts don't improve satisfaction
- **OfficeProducts** has the most consistent quality ratings
- Products with **50%+ discount AND 4.0+ rating** 
  are the sweet spot for customer value
- Only **2 products** are overpriced with poor ratings

## Business Recommendations
1. **CATEGORY FOCUS** → Prioritize Electronics inventory 
   — highest revenue potential across all categories
2. **DISCOUNT STRATEGY** → Don't use heavy discounts to 
   improve ratings. Use discounts to drive volume instead
3. **QUALITY PROMOTION** → Feature OfficeProducts more 
   prominently — most consistent ratings
4. **BEST VALUE CAMPAIGN** → Create a "Best Value" section 
   featuring products with 50%+ discount and 4.0+ rating
5. **QUALITY CONTROL** → Flag and review low rated products 
   with minimal discounts — poor customer experience
